About the Creator

Hello! I'm Michelle Ries. I am a twenty-one year old Senior at Eastern Washington University. My major is in Technical Communications with a minor in Visual Design. Once I graduate, I plan to work for Nintendo as a technical editor/writer for game instruction manuals or as an editor for technical documents in general.

When I am not in school, I am working full-time at a local Title and Escrow Company. Beyond those two careers, I enjoy playing video games, reading, sewing, and cosplaying and prop-making. I'm trying to improve on the last two so I can create gorgeous outfits and weapons for the conventions I attend yearly.

I made this website as my final for Desn368. I wanted to create a webspace to provide information regarding the Eeveelutions in Pokemon, one of my most-loved video game titles.

My personal favourite Eeveelution? Leafeon ❤ - Enjoy!
